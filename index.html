About Me

Hello there! I'm a passionate and aspiring front-end web developer with a keen eye for design and a love for crafting seamless user experiences. My journey into the world of coding began started with test automation in Javascript with Playwright & Cypress, and ever since, I've been on an exciting adventure of turning ideas into interactive and visually appealing websites.
  1. Coding Enthusiast: I thrive on turning complex problems into elegant and intuitive solutions. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I'm constantly honing my skills to stay abreast of the latest industry trends.
  2. User-Centric Approach: I believe in putting the user first. My designs not only look good but also prioritize a smooth and enjoyable user experience. Accessibility and responsiveness are at the core of my development philosophy.
  3. Team Player: I'm a collaborative individual who enjoys working in dynamic team environments. I value open communication, constructive feedback, and the collective effort that goes into building exceptional web applications.
  4. Continuous Learner: The ever-evolving nature of technology excites me. I'm committed to staying updated on emerging technologies and frameworks to ensure that my projects are not only cutting-edge but also optimized for performance.
